Purpose of This Page

This page exists to provide support during a time of widespread uncertainty. You all know what we are talking about. Many things are changing quickly, and for some people, leaving their home feels unsafe and scary.

You are our neighbors, and we want to help. This page provides a way for neighbors who do not feel safe leaving their homes to ask for help with everyday tasks. These can include picking up groceries or supplies, walking dogs, helping with house or yard work like shoveling snow or mowing grass, and other practical tasks that help you stay safely at home.

This group is intentionally small and local. Keeping things this way helps protect everyone involved. If the group grows too large, additional groups can be created to keep things decentralized and discreet. If you are ever unsure how this works, you can contact an admin for guidance. We are doing this because our neighbors need support, and we want to help.

How to Make a Request

1. Using the request template below, post your request in the Signal group. Do not share personal information beyond your first name.

2. A helper will indicate they are taking your request by reacting with an emoji and will then send you a direct message. Helpers only communicate using approved helper usernames.

3. In the direct message, we recommend setting disappearing messages to one week to reduce retained information.

4. Once you have confirmed you are speaking with a verified helper, you may share the specific details of your request. This includes lists, timing, and your final location once you feel safe doing so.

Request Template - First name only (no last names): - Task: - General area (no exact address): - Able to pay (if applicable): - Payment method (if applicable): - Date the task needs to be completed:

Please fill out requests in English if possible, as most helpers communicate in English.

Usernames and Safety

For this group, helpers use Signal usernames that follow a specific format:

the + name

The word “the” must always appear exactly as written, followed by a name of your choosing. The name itself may be in English or another language.

This format makes helpers easy to identify and helps prevent someone from presenting themselves as a helper when they are not approved. Neighbors should not use this format. Using it without approval may result in being removed or blocked from the group.

Do not share personal information with anyone in this group unless you personally know them or they are a verified helper. If someone contacts you claiming to be a helper but their username does not begin with “the”, do not engage and notify an admin.

Helpers and neighbors communicate only through Signal. Signal is used because it offers strong privacy protections and supports disappearing messages.

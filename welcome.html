Purpose of This Page

A lot is changing right now, and for many people, things feel uncertain and scary. This group exists to support people who do not feel safe leaving their homes but still need help getting everyday things done.

If you need assistance with groceries, picking up supplies, walking dogs, shoveling snow, mowing grass, or other basic household tasks, this group is here to help connect you with someone who can assist safely and respectfully.

How to Make a Request

  1. Using the request template below, post your request in the Signal group. At this stage, do not share personal information beyond your first name.
  2. An agent will claim your request by reacting to your message with an emoji and will send you a direct message to coordinate next steps.
  3. In the direct message chat, we strongly recommend enabling disappearing messages set to one week. You can do this by tapping the chat name at the top of the conversation, selecting Disappearing Messages, and choosing 1 week.
  4. Once you are in a private chat, you may share your general location and work out the details of your request, such as grocery lists, pickup locations, timing, or other specifics.

Please fill out requests in English, as most agents speak English.

We Are Here to Help

We recognize that the current situation is difficult and unfair, and we take that seriously. Our goal is to help people stay safe, supported, and connected during a time when many feel vulnerable or isolated.

If you feel unsafe or unsure about handling something on your own, please reach out. This group exists because we believe no one should have to face these challenges alone.
